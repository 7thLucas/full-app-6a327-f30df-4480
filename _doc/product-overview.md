# Product Overview

## Product Name
Quantum x QuantumByte — Landing Page

## What It Is
Simple single-page website with two sections celebrating the Quantum x QuantumByte collaboration.

- **Section 1 — Hello QuantumByte**: Bold hero greeting, brand introduction.
- **Section 2 — We Love Quantum x QuantumByte**: Celebration section packed with heart/love icons expressing affection for the collaboration.

## Users / Audience
Visitors landing on the page — fans, partners, or anyone the Quantum x QuantumByte collaboration is introduced to.

## Brand & Tone
Warm, celebratory, love-forward. Heavy use of heart/love iconography in Section 2. Logo placeholder to be replaced once supplied.

## Positioning
A love letter landing page — minimal, expressive, memorable. No forms, no nav, no CTA friction. Pure brand emotion.

## Scope
- 1 page, 2 sections
- Section 1: "Hello QuantumByte" hero
- Section 2: "We Love Quantum x QuantumByte" with abundant ❤️ icons
- Logo: to be attached and integrated by owner

## Strategic Principles
- Simplicity over complexity
- Emotion over information
- Brand warmth above all
