# Design Guidelines — Quantum x QuantumByte Landing Page

## Color Palette
- Primary: Rose/Pink (#f43f5e, #fb7185)
- Background: White (#ffffff) for Section 1, soft rose (#fff1f2) for Section 2
- Text: Slate-900 (#0f172a) for headings, Slate-600 for body

## Typography
- Headings: font-black, large (text-5xl to text-7xl), tight leading
- Body: font-normal, text-lg, relaxed leading
- Section 1 heading: "Hello QuantumByte" — massive, bold, confident

## Section 2 — Heart Icons
- Use MANY heart emojis and icons: ❤️ 🩷 💕 💗 💖 💓 💝 🫀
- Scatter them around the "We Love Quantum x QuantumByte" text
- Animate some hearts floating up (CSS keyframe animation)
- Make it look festive and abundant — not minimal

## Layout
- Full viewport height per section (min-h-screen)
- Centered content
- No sidebar, no nav, no footer

## Logo
- Display in Section 1
- White background or transparent placement
- Appropriate size — not too large, not too small

## Elevation / Shadow
- Minimal shadows
- Clean, flat design with pops of rose color
